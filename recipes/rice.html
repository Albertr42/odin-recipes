Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear
  2. Add water at a ratio of rice: water of 1:1.5
  3. Bring water to a boil, cover with lid, reduce heat to low, allow to cook for 15 minutes, undisturbed
