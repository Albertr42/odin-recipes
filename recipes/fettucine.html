Ingredients

  • Fettucine
  • Butter
  • Parmiggiano Reggiano
  • Heavy Cream

Steps

  1. Cook pasta and reserve 1 cup of pasta water
  2. In a sauce pot, melt butter and heavy creamy, cut the heat, and mix in cheese, using reserved pasta water to emuslify
  3. Add your sauce to your pasta, using pasta water as needed
